Air Fryer Brussels Sprouts

Air Fryer Brussels Sprouts, you are pure, unbridled, crispy addiction!

To give the Brussels sprouts flair, I took inspiration from Roasted Brussels Sprouts and added some garlic cloves to the mix. The garlic gets very dark and crispy, which we adore. Feel free to use the garlic, skip it, or try one of the other variations below.

INGREDIENTS

  • Brussel sprouts
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic cloves
  • Salt + pepper

DIRECTIONS

  1. Trim and halve the brussel sprouts
  2. (Optional) Soak the sprouts in water for 10 minutes if you’d like them a little softer in the center.
  3. Drain and dry the sprouts. Toss them with oil and spices, then add them to the air fryer.
  4. Cook for 5 minutes at 375 degrees F, then toss. Cook another 5 minutes, then toss again. Add the garlic (if using), and cook for 2 to 4 more minutes.
  5. Add any desired toppings, and ENJOY!
